How to Play

1

Read the vehicle report carefully — year, make, engine, mileage, and the customer's description all matter.

2

Select your diagnosis from the dropdown and press FILE. You have 6 attempts to identify the correct fault.

3

After each wrong guess, a new diagnostic clue is revealed — live data, test results, or inspection findings.

4

The tile grid shows how close each guess was across 8 dimensions: System, Sub-system, Root Cause, Component, Severity, Pattern, Frequency, and Related Fault.

■ Green = exact match   ■ Amber = close   ■ Blue = same family   ■ Red = wrong

A new puzzle drops every day at midnight UTC. Come back tomorrow!

About Mechle

Mechle is a daily car diagnostic puzzle game for mechanics, technicians, and automotive enthusiasts. Each day presents a real-world repair scenario — your job is to identify the root cause in as few guesses as possible.

Cases are based on common real-world diagnostic patterns encountered in professional repair shops. The symptoms are intentionally ambiguous — just like the real thing.

Built with love for the trade. New puzzle every day at midnight UTC.
